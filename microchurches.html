Microchurches

Each microchurch is a missional community—1-40 disciples who gather, grow, and serve around a focused call. All share the same gospel roots while expressing faith in highly contextual ways.

Forested Families

Faith-driven support network for young adults aging out of foster care (forestedfamilies.org). Forested Families offers safe housing, life-skills coaching, and long-term mentoring, surrounding participants with a “family forest” of lasting relationships as they build independent, hope-filled lives.

Cedar Family Table

Hospitality team serving meals, planning activities, and offering relational support to families staying at the Ronald McDonald House of Dayton.
