Microchurches

Each microchurch is a missional community—10-40 disciples who gather, grow, and serve around a focused call. All share the same gospel roots while expressing faith in highly contextual ways.

Forested Families

Outdoor-oriented fellowship for parents & children (see forestedfamilies.org). Monthly hikes, campfire worship, and creation care initiatives.

Cedar Family Table

Hospitality team serving meals, planning activities, and offering relational support to families staying at the local Ronald McDonald House.
