Our Mission

Witness Tree Network exists to proclaim and embody the gospel of Jesus Christ through practical service to people in need. We allocate at least 51 % of all income directly to community outreach—partnering with local nonprofits, supporting teens, providing safe transitional housing, and more.

We model our structure after historic witness trees: a single, deep-rooted life giving rise to many living branches—our microchurches—each bearing fruit in its own unique context.
